How to use

Include the script and the stylesheet

Add the attribute "parax-bg" to the element that should be the background of its parent. It will be absolute positionized

<div class="parax-bg-vp">
    <div parax-bg style="background-image:url(bg.jpg)"></div>
    other content
</div>

The stylesheet and the "parax-bg-vp" class on the parent (the viewport) are optional but recommended
